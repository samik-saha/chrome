Privacy Policy

Gaana Lyrics is aimed at people over 13

User Data

The extension does not collect any user identifiable data. It only collects the artist, album and other track information from the webpage in order to search for lytics online.

Also, the music track related information is collected only for the music websites that the extension supports. For any other website you visit, it does not extract any information from the website.

Gaana lyrics does not store any of the collected information anywhere. The artist/track information is directly sent to online lyrics gathering websites for searching the particular lyrics.
