GAANA LYRICS
BROWSER EXTENSION

Get your song lyrics right on the page for streaming music websites. We support most of the music services on the web.

Works With

Most music services, from Gaana.com to Spotify, Google Music, Pandora, SoundCloud and many others are supported and regularly updated.

Still, if you do not find your favorite music service is not listed, you can just drop us a mail or comment. It will be added as soon as possible.

FREE & OPEN SOURCE

The extension is absolutely free. Also, the source code is available on github
